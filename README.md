# Atliq Hardware — SQL Business Insights Analysis

## Project Overview
This project answers 10 ad-hoc business questions for Atliq Hardware
using MySQL. The analysis covers market presence, product growth,
manufacturing costs, discount strategy, and sales performance
across FY2020 and FY2021.

## Tools Used
- MySQL Workbench
- Gamma (presentation)
- Kling AI (video)

## Database
gdb023 — atliq_hardware_db (6 tables)

## Business Questions Answered
| # | Question | Key Insight |
|---|----------|-------------|
| 1 | APAC markets for Atliq Exclusive | 8 markets — mix of established and emerging |
| 2 | Unique product growth 2020 vs 2021 | 36.33% increase (245 → 334 products) |
| 3 | Product count by segment | Notebooks lead with 129 products |
| 4 | Segment with most growth | Accessories +34 products |
| 5 | Highest & lowest manufacturing cost | 270x range ($0.89 to $240.54) |
| 6 | Top discount customers in India FY2021 | Flipkart leads at 31% |
| 7 | Monthly gross sales — Atliq Exclusive | November peak every year |
| 8 | Best quarter in FY2020 | Q1 (Sep–Nov) — 7M+ units |
| 9 | Channel revenue contribution FY2021 | Retailers dominate at 72.6% |
| 10 | Top 3 products per division FY2021 | N&S leads volume by far |
